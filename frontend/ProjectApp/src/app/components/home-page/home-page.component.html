About The Site

DATSR is a data repository built specifically with time series in mind. We provide a clean and easy interface for users to download and contribute data with the added functionality of a built-in prediction evaluate and score board (for all the competitive engineers out there).

Our Story

Starting out from humble beginnings as an assigned group in a software engineering class at the University of Oregon, DUX D-Zine has now ascended to become the world's leading software development firm after they released their revolutionary application, DATSR.

Owing much of their success to the marvelous work of their professor Juan Flores and GE Kartikeya Sharma, the DUX D-Zine team decided to establish the Flores and Sharma Foundation. The goal of the foundation is to educate the world on responsible software project management.
